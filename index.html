I had lived with DJ these two words for 7 years since 2016. The first EDM music I had ever listened to was in the first years of high school. After that, I went to music festivals and started to understand the different types of genres which can separate into house music, hardstyle, dubstep, bass house, and techno. House music is the one I love after I learn all the genres.

In 2016, I was starting to learn Logic Pro from Youtube and trying to follow the steps and make the same music back by myself. After all the videos and practice, I became very familiar with all the digital instruments and effects, I was starting to create some original music and published it on a website called SoundCloud.

In 2017, I decided to learn to become a DJ. I really wanted to stand on the stage in person and make the crows go crazy when I saw those famous DJs performing on stage and controlling the vibe to climax. After that, I bought the DJ controller and the software to start learning by myself on Youtube. After 6 months, I can already make any kind of music styles set and ready to perform on stage. In the summer, I had been to Berklee of music college to study more about composition until the end of the years. I also join the DJ club to learn and share some skills with other members. After all the experiences, I want back to my country Taiwan and start to perform on club and party.

From 2018 to the present, I play more than 100 times on the stage in different places. Those experiences make me learn more about how to play the right music at the right moment to bring the atmosphere high and crazy. It is the best choose to have me on your club or party to make audiences crazy and having fun.
